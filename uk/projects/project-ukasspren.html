Asspren Tobacco

UKWW Group of Companies

Is there anyone in the world who has not heard of Asspren cigarettes? This famous brand, is characterised by their unique blend of natural tobaccos, and are shipped worldwide for your enjoyment. Manufactured by the UK Worldwide Group, Asspren has quickly become one of the best selling cigarette brands in the Middle East, as shown by the 2015 national survey in which it tied, in high position, with another famous cigarette brand.
