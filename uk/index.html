About Us

Founded in 2003 by Dr Hallow Talabani, the UK WORLDWIDE GROUP was established initially in London. It was built upon, and has always retained the tenets of spirit, grit, innovation and perseverance.

Mission

To focus on our customers’ market challenges and needs by providing excellent solutions and services in order to consistently create maximum value for our customers.

Vision

To be a premium global conglomerate, with a clear focus on each of our businesses.

Goal & Objective

We aim for the deployment of Science and Technology in the design, development and deivery of high quality and globally marketable products and services
